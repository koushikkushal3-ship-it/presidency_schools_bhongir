Message from Chairperson

Mrs. Jayalaxmi Teegala

“Learning is the only thing the mind never exhausts, never fears, and never regrets.” – Leonardo da Vinci

Having been a part of the education sector for over over two decades now, I firmly believe that quality of education plays a prominent role in the future of society, nation-building and overall development of humankind.

It is crucial to build an excellent educational system to deliver such education, especially in rural areas. Only the best educational organisations can sustain the rapidly growing expectations of a fast-moving world.

Today's parents are more quality inclined. They believe that only the finest schools can perfectly mould their children and prepare them to face the world without compromising their values.

Keeping the contemporary global and national perspective in our mind, we strive to achieve excellence in the grooming and upbringing of a child to the best of their potential.
